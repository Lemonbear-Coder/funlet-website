Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device

Overview

Funlet is designed to work fully offline. Your game progress, settings, and scores are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

What we collect

Funlet does not collect any personal information. We do not require account creation, and we do not ask for your name, email, or any identifying details.

App Store analytics (such as download counts and crash reports) may be collected by Apple as part of the standard App Store experience. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data storage

All app data including game progress, scores, and settings is stored locally on your device using standard browser storage. Uninstalling the app removes this data entirely.

Third parties

Funlet does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Children

Funlet does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. The app contains no accounts, no chat, and no network communication.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@funlet.fun.
